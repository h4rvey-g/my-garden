Seborrheic keratosis is a <span style="background:rgba(240, 200, 0, 0.2)">benign growth of immature keratinocytes.</span>
# Epidemiology
---
Seborrheic keratosis is the most common benign skin tumor in adults > 30 years of age.

# Etiology
---
