A form of hemoglobin formed by Î³-globin chains that develop when there is a deficiency of alpha globin chains in alpha thalassemias. It is most pronounced when all 4 alleles for alpha globulin are missing, so-called Hb Barts disease, and leads to <span style="background:rgba(240, 200, 0, 0.2)">hydrops fetalis</span>.
