- **Anabolic**
	- **Meaning:** “Building up” processes.
	- **ANA-** = **Add/Ascent** (building up)
- **Catabolic**
	- **Meaning:** “Breaking down” processes.
	- **CATA-** = **Collapse/Crash** (breaking down)
